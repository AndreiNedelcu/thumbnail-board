The creator discusses his decade-long practice of eating nearly identical meals daily as a deliberate strategy to reduce decision fatigue and simplify his lifestyle. His consistent diet consists of a protein source (chicken, fish, or beef), rice, and green vegetables, supplemented by a daily green smoothie he's maintained for ten years—all designed to support his fitness goals of consuming approximately 160 grams of protein daily to aid strength training. He explains that automating food choices eliminates the cognitive burden of meal planning, grocery shopping, and recipe research, allowing him to focus mental energy on more important priorities. While acknowledging this approach isn't for everyone—particularly those who derive joy from culinary exploration—he argues its practical benefits include effortless shopping, reduced cooking complexity, and easier macro-nutrient tracking. To maintain some variety without sacrificing consistency, he allocates roughly 20 percent of meals to deviation, occasionally eating different breakfasts or dining out twice weekly where he explores diverse cuisines. The video targets productivity-focused individuals and minimalism enthusiasts interested in optimizing daily routines and reducing decision fatigue through habit automation.
