Comparisons to Larry Bird via advanced metrics validated the organization's commitment to building around him. The narrative emphasizes how Jokic's dedication—from losing thirty pounds to his humble demeanor—combined with coaching that emphasized decision-making over punishing mistakes, allowed him to average triple-doubles and become a franchise centerpiece despite skepticism about his unconventional skill set.
