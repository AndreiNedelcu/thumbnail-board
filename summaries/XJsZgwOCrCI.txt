This is a "YouTube Poop" style parody that remixes scenes and dialogue from Finding Nemo into absurdist comedy through aggressive editing, audio splicing, and non-sequiturs. The video rapidly jumps between altered movie moments, inserting random phrases, memes, and crude humor—including jokes about killing characters, inappropriate questions, and self-referential commentary that breaks the film's narrative logic. Key scenes like Coral's death, Nemo's capture, and Marlin's journey are deconstructed and reassembled with jarring transitions, sound effects, and unexpected dialogue replacements that create deliberately cringe-inducing humor. The parody targets viewers familiar with both the original Pixar film and internet culture, particularly those who enjoy absurdist meme formats and heavily edited video remixes that deconstruct mainstream media. The comedic style relies on shock value, timing, and the contrast between the wholesome source material and the chaotic, crude edits layered over it.
