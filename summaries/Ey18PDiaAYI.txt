The instructor, who claims to have generated over half a million dollars in revenue through AI agent development, walks through setting up a free N8N trial and establishing credentials to create 15+ functional AI automations. Key topics include APIs, HTTP requests, AI agent architecture with memory systems, multi-agent configurations, webhook triggers, MCP servers, and practical prompting techniques.
