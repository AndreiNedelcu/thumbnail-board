This Adobe Photoshop tutorial demonstrates how to create a viral-style social media poster using modern design techniques and trending aesthetics. The instructor walks through the complete workflow, starting with document setup and canvas organization using artboards, then building a layered composition featuring a cyan-toned background with soft brushwork, strategically positioned clock imagery, a central subject photo with selective saturation adjustments, geometric shapes created with the pen tool, and bold typography reading "more win less loss this week" with perspective transformation effects. Key design elements include color overlays, clipping masks for shadow effects, opacity adjustments for depth, and hashtag text layering. The tutorial targets beginner to intermediate graphic designers and content creators looking to master professional poster design in Photoshop, covering essential tools like the brush, eraser, type tool, and blending modes. The presenter emphasizes organizational best practices through layer grouping and color-coding, while also promoting a graphic design resource pack containing templates, fonts, and stock images to streamline the creative process.
