# Watches and Wonders 2024 Review

This video captures an on-the-ground review of the Watches and Wonders annual trade show in Geneva, where major luxury watch brands debut their latest releases. The presenter tours booths from industry giants including Rolex, IWC, TAG Heuer, and Frederico Buccellati, examining new models and interviewing brand representatives. Key launches discussed include Rolex's new calibers and updated designs like the yellow gold Tiffany Daytona and the 1908, IWC's Engineer collection with perpetual calendar complications, and TAG Heuer's renewed Formula 1 partnership featuring solar-powered chronographs. A notable centerpiece is the presenter's custom Frederico Buccellati worldtimer featuring a rare bloodstone dial, a two-and-a-half-year project with hand-polished components and engravings of his children. The video targets passionate watch enthusiasts and collectors who follow industry trends, technical specifications, and brand heritage, while balancing serious critique with accessibility discussions, particularly regarding pricing and real-world wearability. Throughout, the presenter provides candid opinions on design choices, complications, and value propositions across different price points.
