This video documents a professional dog groomer undertaking the challenge of grooming a 220-pound Caucasian Shepherd (Ovcharka) that multiple other groomers had refused to work with. The dog, known for the breed's aggressive tendencies and territorial instincts, presents significant logistical challenges due to his refusal to stand on a grooming table or enter a bathtub, requiring the groomer to work on the floor with a walk-in shower. The groomer emphasizes the importance of reading body language and understanding individual temperament before working with large, potentially dangerous dogs, noting this particular dog is unusually friendly and laid-back despite breed stereotypes. The groom takes approximately two and a half hours and involves bathing with specialized shampoo, blow-drying, brushing, nail trimming, and selective shaving of matted areas beneath the chin. The video targets dog grooming professionals and enthusiasts interested in handling large or challenging breeds, while also resonating with dog owners seeking specialized grooming services for their pets. The transformation ultimately brings tears of joy to the dog's owner, who had struggled to find groomers willing to accept her pet.
