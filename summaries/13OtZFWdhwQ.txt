This video documents an ambitious attempt to capture footage of urban downhill mountain bike racing using advanced FPV drone technology. The challenge involves following Thomas Slavic, a legendary street racer, through the treacherous Red Bull Cro Bajo course in Valparaiso, Chile, where tight urban terrain creates severe signal interference. The Dutch drone pilots employ innovative solutions including directional antennas and a relay drone equipped with signal boosters to maintain video feed through narrow streets, steep staircases, and high-rise buildings. The production captures the technical complexity and stress of real-time coordination between multiple pilots, as wind conditions, signal dropout, and razor-thin margins for error test both the riders and drone operators throughout practice runs and the final race. The video targets extreme sports enthusiasts and drone technology followers, showcasing cutting-edge aerial cinematography while documenting the operational challenges of capturing professional-grade footage in demanding, unpredictable environments.
