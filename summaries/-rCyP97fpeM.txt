# Summary

This video documents the behind-the-scenes creation of an ambitious visual effects project featuring a giant Mario character chasing Luigi through real-world locations. The creator leverages Gaussian Splats—a machine learning technology that captures 3D photorealistic environments in real-time—combined with first-person perspective filming to simulate a giant's viewpoint without traditional CGI. The project involves extensive drone footage of various locations, combined with handheld camera work to create the illusion of a colossal character moving through real spaces. A significant portion focuses on the creator's struggle with using a smartphone for head-mounted footage, which resulted in excessive camera shake and vibration, forcing a complete reshoot with a GoPro. The video explores the technical and creative challenges of maintaining consistent scale, choreographing performances in pre-captured environments, and coordinating with team members for final shots. The creator emphasizes the emotional toll of the four-month solo production process and the value of embracing the creative work despite self-doubt. This content targets filmmakers, VFX enthusiasts, and viewers interested in innovative approaches to blending practical effects with emerging technology.
