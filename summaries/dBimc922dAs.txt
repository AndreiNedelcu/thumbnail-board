This video reviews the best drawing apps for iPad in 2024, aimed at both professional artists and casual users. The presenter covers Procreate, Adobe Fresco, Adobe Illustrator, Affinity Designer, Apple Notes, Notability, and Clip Studio Paint, evaluating each based on features, pricing models, and specific use cases. Key topics include brush customization capabilities, vector versus bitmap drawing, ruler and precision tools, animation features through apps like Procreate Dreams and Clip Studio Paint's animation suite, and integration with Creative Cloud services. The video emphasizes that Adobe Fresco has become the presenter's preferred daily driver over Procreate due to superior precision tools and live paint simulation, though Procreate remains excellent for general illustration. Other standout features discussed include Affinity Designer as a one-time purchase alternative to subscriptions, Apple Notes as a free basic option with surprising functionality, and Notability for students combining note-taking with drawing. The content targets iPad users ranging from professional graphic designers and digital artists to students and hobbyists seeking to understand which application best suits their specific creative workflow and budget constraints.
