# Summary

Anthropic has launched Claude Fable 5 (also known as Claude 3.5 Sonnet), a powerful new AI model that significantly surpasses existing systems like GPT-4.5 and Claude Opus 4.8 across multiple benchmarks, with exceptional capabilities in software engineering, research, and reasoning. The model features extended context windows of 300,000 tokens and includes extended thinking capabilities, though a notable feature allows users to disable thinking mode. The video demonstrates Fable 5's practical applications through concrete examples: generating HTML/CSS/JavaScript games like platformers and Pokémon gameplay, creating interactive 3D simulations of solar systems and traffic scenarios, and building custom React implementations—all with remarkable speed despite high token consumption costs. However, access is limited; while available through the API starting immediately, Fable 5 will only be included free in subscription plans until June 22, 2025, after which usage requires paid credits due to expected high demand. The host notes the paradox of Anthropic simultaneously advocating for slowing AI development while releasing their most powerful model, questioning whether labs should collectively pause frontier AI research to allow society to adapt. The video targets developers and AI enthusiasts interested in understanding cutting-edge model capabilities and practical applications.
