# Summary

The video features an interview with Jordan Schwarzenberger, CEO of Arcade Media, who manages the Sidemen—a British YouTube group with nearly 20 million subscribers that has built a multi-million-dollar entertainment empire. Schwarzenberger explains how he and his co-founders Sam (an accountant who worked with the group for eight years) and Aaron (also an accountant) positioned themselves as a dedicated management company focused on expanding the Sidemen's brand beyond content creation. The interview explores how the Sidemen have successfully diversified into ventures including an alcohol brand, trading cards, a membership platform, a podcast, retail stores, restaurants, and live events like their annual charity football match that draws thousands of fans. Schwarzenberger describes their unique democratic decision-making process where all seven members vote on initiatives, with majority rule determining approval. He draws on his previous experience at Vice, Lab Bible, and YMU (Europe's largest talent management company) to apply strategic IP exploitation and scaling expertise. The conversation targets aspiring creators and entrepreneurs interested in understanding how content creators can build sustainable business ecosystems, manage creative talent, and navigate the evolving creator economy landscape.
