This tutorial guides users through setting up an AI customer agent within the Claio platform. The video covers the complete onboarding process, starting with the setup wizard that automatically scrapes product information, policies, and FAQs from Shopify stores to build the agent's knowledge foundation. It explains how to optionally add external knowledge bases from help centers, configure handoff procedures when the agent cannot answer questions, and connect to help desk platforms like Zendesk or Gorgeous to preserve conversation context. The content emphasizes essential setup steps presented as a checklist, including testing the agent in the playground with sample customer questions before going live. Users learn how to enable communication channels such as web chat, SMS, and email to deploy the agent across customer touchpoints. The video targets Claio platform users who want to implement conversational AI support, particularly e-commerce businesses seeking to automate customer service while maintaining quality responses and ensuring seamless escalation to human agents when necessary.
