This video presents a comprehensive drag race comparison between three ultra-high-performance Brabus vehicles: the 900 Rocket R based on a Porsche 911 Turbo S, and two variants of the Brabus 900 Super Black based on the Mercedes-AMG GLS63 and G63. Each vehicle has been tuned to produce 900 horsepower, though with different power delivery characteristics and torque limitations. Host Matt Watson and guests conduct multiple racing formats including standing quarter-mile races and rolling acceleration tests from various speeds. The Rocket R emerges as the clear performance winner, completing the quarter mile in 10 seconds flat compared to 11.3 seconds for the GLS and 11.5 seconds for the G63. Throughout the video, the hosts discuss launch characteristics, handling dynamics, exhaust acoustics, and subjective driving experiences. The content targets automotive enthusiasts and performance car fans interested in comparing extreme luxury tuner vehicles, with emphasis on real-world performance metrics, driver feedback, and the visceral experience of operating these bespoke hypercars.
