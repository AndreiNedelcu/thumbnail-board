Motion is an AI-powered scheduling and task management application designed to consolidate tasks, projects, and calendars into a single platform. The core feature uses artificial intelligence to automatically reorganize and prioritize tasks based on user-defined attributes such as project, priority, duration, deadline, and working hours, while integrating with Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCloud. Beyond task automation, Motion offers project management capabilities with Gantt charts, list views, and filtering options, team collaboration features for multiple users, and convenient tools like booking availability links and built-in video conferencing. The application emphasizes a minimal, intuitive design and includes additional productivity features such as task timers, soft/hard deadlines, and the ability to disable auto-scheduling for unmovable tasks. Priced at $19 monthly for individuals or $12 per user monthly for teams, Motion targets professionals and small teams seeking to reduce productivity administration overhead and context-switching by centralizing scheduling and task organization. The platform appeals particularly to those who want AI-assisted workflow optimization without managing multiple separate applications.
