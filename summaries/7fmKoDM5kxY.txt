# Summary

The video presents a first-impression review of Notion Mail, a newly released email application from Notion that aims to integrate email management into the Notion ecosystem. The reviewer tests the web-based and native Mac versions, documenting the onboarding process, core features, and limitations. Key features discussed include a minimalist interface with Gmail integration, customizable inbox views and filters, slash commands, email templates that pull calendar invites, keyboard shortcuts, light/dark mode options, and AI capabilities (though not yet enabled). The reviewer identifies several issues: the app currently only supports Gmail, adding multiple email accounts encountered bugs, email images don't auto-load, and nested folder structures aren't available. The video targets Notion power users and those seeking integrated productivity tools, but the reviewer concludes that Notion Mail, while promising for beta release, isn't yet a complete email replacement for multi-account users due to account access limitations. Apple Mail, Gmail, and Outlook remain more versatile alternatives for those managing multiple email addresses.
