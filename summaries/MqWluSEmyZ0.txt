# Summary

This tutorial explains how to monetize TikTok through affiliate marketing, focusing on three primary methods. The first and most lucrative approach leverages TikTok Shop's native affiliate program, where creators can tag products directly in videos and earn commissions ranging from $5 to $25+ per sale. Creators can request free product samples to review and promote without upfront costs. To access this feature, users must be at least 18 years old, comply with FTC regulations, and ideally have 5,000 followers, though alternative onboarding methods exist for smaller accounts. The second method involves creating a link-in-bio storefront using platforms like Linktree or Wix to promote external affiliate products. The third method uses custom trackable URLs displayed on-screen, allowing creators to use the same content across multiple platforms like Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts while maintaining affiliate tracking capabilities. Throughout, the creator emphasizes the importance of legal compliance, FTC disclosure requirements, and maintaining audience trust through transparent labeling of affiliate relationships. The guide targets aspiring TikTok creators seeking to monetize their content through performance-based marketing opportunities.
