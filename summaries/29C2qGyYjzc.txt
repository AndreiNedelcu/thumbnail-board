# Summary

This discussion with LinkedIn strategist Jasmin Alic explores the platform's significant 2024-2025 evolution, covering the shift from static content toward video and storytelling while maintaining LinkedIn's text-based foundation. The speaker emphasizes that despite LinkedIn's push toward vertical video to compete with TikTok, text-only posts still generate superior reach and engagement compared to images. Key strategic insights include understanding that LinkedIn's 1 billion+ user base has less than 2% active creators, creating an opportunity for individuals willing to share authentic, niche-specific content. The conversation details tactical approaches including analyzing dwell time and comment engagement as critical algorithm factors, the effectiveness of pinned comments to foster community dialogue, and why content strategy must align with specific business goals rather than arbitrary posting quotas. Alic stresses that success requires understanding your particular audience, region, and niche rather than applying generic strategies, and that authentic engagement metrics like comments and sustained post interaction matter more than vanity metrics like likes. The content targets professionals, entrepreneurs, course creators, and anyone seeking to build influence and business results on LinkedIn through data-driven, strategic content creation.
