This video analyzes Drake's problematic relationship with women, tracing how his "nice guy" image has eroded over years into allegations of misogyny and predatory behavior. The creator examines his public pursuit of WNBA player Skylar Diggins via cringeworthy poetry at the ESPYs, his one-sided obsession with Rihanna, and his pattern of targeting significantly younger women including Hailey Bieber (whom he knew at 14), Millie Bobby Brown (13), Billie Eilish, and influencer Aya Tanjali. The video highlights controversial lyrics from "Jaded" allegedly about Georgia Smith and documents his casual dismissal of Sophie Brussaux's pregnancy. Additional allegations include his alleged affair with Lil Wayne's girlfriend while Wayne was incarcerated despite Wayne's mentorship, his defense of bodyguard Baka (convicted of assault), and pattern statements by comedians predicting his downfall years before Kendrick Lamar's recent accusations. The video targets hip-hop audiences interested in celebrity criticism and uses mounting evidence from interviews, lyrics analysis, and third-party accounts to argue Drake has systematically shown disregard for women's autonomy and dignity regardless of their age or relationship to him.
