# Summary

This video presents a comprehensive guide to improving typing speed from an average to 150+ words per minute in five days through six evidence-based exercises. The host, a 150 wpm typist from the MKBHD studio, tests three unconventional techniques on volunteers Tim and Adam: using a weighted keyboard to build muscle memory (inspired by baseball bat warm-up theory), visual distraction exercises to improve focus and accuracy, and rhythm-based typing with synchronized music to enhance flow state. The video then demonstrates three practical methods anyone can use at home: keybr.com for personalized keyboard training based on weak areas, MonkeyType's specialized modes like "read ahead" to build word-muscle memory, and curated music at matched tempos to maintain typing rhythm. The host concludes with real-world results from studio members who spent 20 minutes daily practicing these methods over one work week, with all participants improving significantly and three breaking 100 wpm for the first time. The content targets typing enthusiasts, office workers, programmers, and anyone seeking rapid productivity improvement through practical, science-informed techniques.
