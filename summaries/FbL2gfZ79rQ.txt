The Samsung Frame TV is presented as a premium entertainment solution that prioritizes design alongside functionality. The 65-inch 4K QLED display features quantum dot technology, HDR 10+ support, and a matte finish that eliminates glare while displaying vibrant, accurate colors. The standout feature is Art Mode, which transforms the TV into a digital canvas displaying over 2,300 artworks from the Samsung store or personal uploads when not watching content. The TV includes multiple installation options—standard feet, wall mount, or the distinctive Studio stand—paired with interchangeable magnetic frames in various finishes to match any décor. The setup includes Samsung's One Connect box for cable management and a premium remote with solar charging. Audio capabilities are enhanced by the optional S801B ultra-slim soundbar and subwoofer, featuring 3.1.2 channels, Dolby Atmos, and Q Symphony technology for synchronized TV and soundbar output. The software provides access to streaming apps, Samsung TV Plus for free content, and game streaming capabilities. This comprehensive setup targets design-conscious consumers and home entertainment enthusiasts seeking a television that doubles as interior décor while maintaining excellent picture and sound quality.
