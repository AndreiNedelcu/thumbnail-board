This video is a comprehensive guide to the college application process designed for high school students navigating the admissions system. The creator breaks down the entire journey into six main stages: building a college list, starting applications, writing essays, obtaining recommendation letters, applying for financial aid, and submitting applications. The video emphasizes practical strategies like categorizing schools into safety, match, and reach categories (typically three to five matches, two to three reaches, and around three safeties), though acknowledges that many competitive students "shotgun" applications to prestigious schools. Key decision factors discussed include college size, class structure, location (urban versus rural), available programs, campus culture, extracurricular opportunities, and cost analysis through net price calculators and ROI calculations. The content covers application platforms like the Common App and Coalition App, explains early action versus early decision deadlines, and provides tools like Google sheets templates for tracking schools. Targeting high school juniors and seniors preparing for college admissions, the video combines strategic advice with logistical guidance, drawing on the creator's personal experience and references to institutions like MIT and Harvard to illustrate various concepts.
