This video exposes alleged cryptocurrency pump-and-dump schemes involving KSI, a major YouTuber, uncovered through blockchain analysis by crypto investigator Zach XBT. The investigation reveals a pattern where KSI publicly expressed bullish sentiment on tokens like XCAD and EARN while simultaneously selling hundreds of thousands of dollars worth to his millions of followers, causing trading volume spikes before the coins' prices collapsed. Evidence includes wallet transactions dated immediately after promotional tweets, with KSI claiming he was inexperienced, lost money overall, and even tweeted the opposite of his actual positions thinking the market worked against him. KSI's explanations range from admitting profit-taking to blaming poor market timing, but investigators argue these actions constitute textbook pump-and-dump manipulation regardless of intent. The video targets audiences interested in cryptocurrency fraud, influencer accountability, and financial exploitation, presenting detailed transaction data and timestamps to substantiate allegations while giving KSI opportunity to respond.
