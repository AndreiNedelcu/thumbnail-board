# Summary

This video demonstrates how to create a personalized AI chatbot trained on a specific person's knowledge using Mind Studio, a no-code platform for building AI applications. The creator builds "BeastBot," an AI assistant modeled after MrBeast by collecting and uploading 11 publicly available interview transcripts and documentary content into the platform's data source system. The process involves extracting YouTube transcripts, organizing them as text files, setting up initial personalization questions to gather context about the user, writing detailed system instructions to define the bot's tone and expertise, and configuring a workflow that expands user questions, queries the training data, and generates responses using language models like GPT-4 or Claude. The technique is universally applicable—users can create specialized AI advisors for any field by sourcing relevant content. Mind Studio currently operates in beta offering free bot creation with optional paid premium features, and creators can monetize their bots through subscription fees. The video targets aspiring YouTubers and content creators interested in leveraging AI for growth advice, though the methodology applies to various professional domains from mechanics to culinary arts.
