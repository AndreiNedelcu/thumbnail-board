This tutorial demonstrates how to train a custom Flux AI model to generate images with your own face using free credits. The creator walks through using Replicate's platform with Luca Taco's AI toolkit to train a LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) model on personal headshots, requiring a minimum of 12-20 images renamed with a trigger word like "Mr E flow." The process involves creating a HuggingFace token, uploading images as a zip file, and training for approximately 25 minutes. The video addresses cost concerns by revealing a $10 coupon code from Replicate (approximately 86 free image generations), making the typically $2-3 training cost effectively free. The creator then demonstrates prompt optimization using Claude's custom instructions to generate higher-quality images, emphasizing that placing the trigger word first in prompts yields better results. Additional sections show integration with Runway for video animation and comparisons with other models like Midjourney. The content targets AI enthusiasts, content creators, and hobbyists interested in affordable personalized AI image generation without paid subscriptions.
