# Video Summary

This video compares Toast and Owner.com as online ordering and website solutions for restaurants, analyzing when each platform makes sense. Toast functions as a cheap, integrated add-on for restaurants already using its POS system, costing approximately $200 monthly for website, online ordering, and email marketing modules. However, Toast requires restaurants to build and optimize websites from scratch, demanding knowledge of SEO, conversion rate optimization, and design best practices. Owner.com costs over $500 monthly but delivers a proven, science-backed system designed specifically to maximize sales through split-tested designs and guest experience optimization. Owner.com differentiates itself through features like branded mobile apps (mimicking industry leaders like Starbucks and Chipotle), loyalty programs, push notifications, SMS marketing, and AI-generated menu pages for search engine visibility. The platform strategically converts customers away from third-party delivery apps like DoorDash and Uber Eats by offering faster service, loyalty rewards, and transparent fee savings. While Toast suits restaurant owners seeking simplicity and low costs, Owner.com targets growth-focused operators prioritizing sales increases, customer relationship recovery, and reduced delivery app fees. The video notes Owner.com integrates with Toast's POS, allowing restaurants to use both systems simultaneously.
