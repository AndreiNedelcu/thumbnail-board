# Summary

This video documents a professional dog groomer's visit to a grooming salon in Carmel, California, where she grooms two dogs associated with the Equine Healing Collaborative, a rescue sanctuary farm that provides animal-assisted psychotherapy services. The groomer bathes and grooms Finn, a large working Kangal livestock guardian dog, and Charlie, a mini doodle visiting a professional groomer for the first time. Throughout the grooming process, the groomer encounters challenges including tick removal on Finn, difficulty getting the anxious dogs to cooperate, managing heavy shedding, and working with unfamiliar salon equipment. The video emphasizes the farm's therapeutic mission, where rescue animals are integrated into mental health treatment sessions with licensed therapists, while also highlighting that the facility is at financial risk due to property being for sale. The content targets dog grooming enthusiasts and animal lovers interested in both professional grooming techniques and animal welfare causes, with calls to support the farm's fundraising efforts embedded throughout.
