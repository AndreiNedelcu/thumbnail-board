This video provides a comprehensive buyer's guide for the iPhone 16 lineup, helping consumers navigate purchasing decisions across four closely-priced models. The presenter details critical distinctions that Apple downplayed, including battery life improvements favoring Pro models, display differences (60Hz versus 120Hz ProMotion), and physical size increases in Pro phones. Key technical differences highlighted include USB 3 speeds on Pro models versus USB 2.0 on standard phones, camera system variations with dedicated zoom lenses only on Pro versions, and Pro Raw/Pro Res exclusivity. The video emphasizes that while the A18 and A18 Pro chips perform similarly, Apple Intelligence features won't launch until fall and won't function on older iPhone models. The presenter argues the Plus model offers poor value given its $100 proximity to the Pro, recommends avoiding older iPhone 15 and 14 models despite lower prices, and suggests the primary decision factors are size preference and camera capability. Targeting potential iPhone upgraders, the guide warns against common purchasing mistakes and stresses that flagship features like Apple Intelligence justify buying newer models for long-term software support.
