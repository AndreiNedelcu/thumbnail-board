This video examines the dramatic fall of Manchester United winger Antony from promising prospect to controversial figure and underperforming player. It traces his journey from São Paulo through Ajax, where he impressed with goals and assists in the 2021-22 season and won an Olympic gold medal with Brazil's U23 squad, to his £85 million transfer to Manchester United under Erik ten Hag. Initially promising with goals in his first three matches, Antony's form collapsed dramatically—he went six months without scoring and managed only 11 goals in 83 matches overall. The video details his poor performances in the 2022 World Cup, an altercation with Jeremy Doku that drew social media mockery, and his tendency toward showboating with questionable skills like a pointless 360-spin move rather than effective play. His predictable left-foot cutting inside approach makes him easy for defenders to neutralize, and his lack of pace prevents him from reaching the byline. The video, targeting football fans and Manchester United supporters, argues that Antony's expensive transfer has proven disappointing, turning him into an internet joke while the midfielder struggles to justify his price tag.
