Anatoly, a competitive powerlifter, conducts a prank video where he disguises himself as a gym cleaner carrying a 32kg mop to trick gym-goers into believing the heavy object is just cleaning equipment. Throughout the video, he uses this disguise to approach various gym members performing exercises and offers unsolicited form corrections and training advice, often demonstrating alternative techniques using the weighted mop. The gym members initially dismiss his suggestions, skeptical of a "cleaner" giving coaching tips, but gradually become impressed and confused when he performs impressive feats of strength with the mop, such as holding it in challenging positions and performing compound movements. The prank plays on the contrast between his muscular appearance, athletic ability, and the menial job he's pretending to hold, creating humorous reactions from gym patrons who cannot reconcile his evident strength with his janitorial role. The video targets fitness enthusiasts and prank content audiences, combining humor with fitness education as Anatoly demonstrates proper exercise technique and promotes his power-building training program throughout the video.
