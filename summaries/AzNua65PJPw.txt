This video documents the professional grooming of Princess Smoochie, a ten-year-old King Shepherd that hasn't been professionally groomed in a decade and arrived heavily matted and dirty. The groomer works through an extensive bathing process using specialized deshedding and deep-cleaning shampoos, followed by high-velocity blow-drying to remove compacted undercoat and loose hair rather than shaving the dog entirely. Throughout the process, the groomer shares insights about why she chose specific techniques—bathing before brushing to avoid pain, using the dryer to loosen matting rather than attempting to brush compacted fur, and explaining that the dog's condition is typical for farm dogs exposed to outdoor elements. After two hours and forty-five minutes of work, Princess Smoochie transforms dramatically, emerging clean, fluffy, and far more comfortable. The video targets dog grooming enthusiasts and pet owners interested in professional grooming techniques, featuring an emotional owner reaction at the end that emphasizes the remarkable before-and-after transformation and the dog's surprisingly well-behaved temperament throughout the challenging grooming session.
