# Summary

This video presents three Instagram strategies specifically designed to drive sales for restaurants rather than just accumulating followers and engagement metrics. The primary strategy is paid retargeting through Facebook Pixel and ads, which targets previous website visitors who abandoned their online orders—a surprisingly large audience since most ordering systems only convert 20% of visitors. The second strategy involves optimizing Instagram profiles with clear descriptions of cuisine type, differentiating factors, calls-to-action, and story highlights that answer customer questions about hours, popular dishes, and ordering options. The third strategy covers organic content creation that combines high-quality food photography with consistent posting and strategic use of filters to boost engagement and visibility on explore pages and geotagged locations. The video emphasizes that likes and impressions are meaningless without actual sales conversions, advocating for entertainment-focused content that builds brand connection while using location tags to reach local customers actively searching for dining options. Real restaurant examples like Brooklyn Pizza and Taco's demonstrate these strategies' effectiveness in driving hundreds of monthly orders and thousands in additional revenue.
