# LTM 1750 Mobile Telescopic Crane Manufacturing

This video documents the complete construction of the LTM 1750, the world's largest road-capable mobile crane, manufactured by Liebherr in Germany. The video follows the crane's assembly from metal fabrication through final testing, showcasing the intricate engineering required to build this 108-ton machine capable of lifting 800 tons. Key components discussed include the nine-axle carrier chassis with four driven and all steerable axles, the 52-meter telescopic boom reaching heights of 152 meters with attachments, the rotating superstructure with dual steering systems enabling extreme maneuverability, the 680-horsepower in-house V8 diesel engine, and sophisticated hydraulic systems managing suspension, braking, and lifting operations. The production process emphasizes precision welding, hydraulic accumulator installation, complex steering linkages, and comprehensive testing of all systems. The video targets industrial engineering enthusiasts, crane operators, and those interested in heavy equipment manufacturing, detailing how approximately 4,000 Liebherr workers produce six cranes daily through meticulous assembly coordination and quality control.
