A professional mountain biker attempts to complete an entire trick line on a moving train in an unprecedented stunt. The rider must navigate a custom-built course featuring creative obstacles including step-ups, boxes, step-downs, and a flat drop back flip—a trick never before landed on a mountain bike. The challenge involves three major obstacles: dangerous crosswinds that could blow the rider off the course, maintaining synchronized speed between the train and rider, and executing increasingly difficult tricks with precision. The narrow space makes spins and 360s particularly challenging, and the moving train creates disorienting sensations that affect balance and air awareness. After multiple failed attempts where the rider misses landings and slips on various tricks, he ultimately succeeds in completing the entire run in one continuous take while the train is moving. The video targets adventure sports enthusiasts and extreme sports fans interested in innovative, record-breaking athletic feats and the technical challenges involved in executing dangerous stunts in unconventional locations.
