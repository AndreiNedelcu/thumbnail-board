This video documents a DIY project where the team at Linus Media Group builds a two-person acoustic call pod from scratch to compare against commercial solutions like the Bureau Tuesday Plus ($14,000+). The project spans multiple days of construction, involving detailed woodworking, soundproofing with SonaPan material, double-paned acrylic windows, electrical work, and upholstery. The build uses standard materials including 2x4 framing, hardboard, spray adhesive, and acoustic fabric, alongside tools like table saws, multi-tools, and 3D-printed templates. The finished pod achieves approximately 30dB noise reduction, matching commercial products. The final cost reached $3,100 USD in materials but required 176 hours of labor, making the economics unviable unless done by a company with in-house skilled workers or unlimited personal time. The video targets makers, DIY enthusiasts, and business decision-makers considering workspace solutions, demonstrating both the feasibility and impracticality of custom building office infrastructure.
