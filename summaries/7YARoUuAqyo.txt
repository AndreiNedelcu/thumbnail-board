A founder CEO shares a curated collection of books that shaped his journey to building a $100 million business, organized into four progressive stages of development. The first tier focuses on personal transformation and mindset, including *The War of Art*, *Miracle Morning*, *Tribe of Millionaires*, and *The 48 Laws of Power*, which address emotional resilience and self-awareness essential for entrepreneurship. The second tier covers foundational business education—*The Millionaire Real Estate Agent*, *21 Laws of Leadership*, and *The Personal MBA*—teaching revenue growth, employee development, and business mechanics typically missing from formal education. The third tier emphasizes operational mastery and scaling with *Traction*, *Simple Numbers Straight Talk Big Profits*, *Get Things Done*, and *Unreasonable Hospitality*, focusing on systems, financial literacy, and culture building. The final tier targets enterprise-level growth with *Venture Deals*, *Blueprint to a Billion*, and books on family wealth preservation for founders pursuing significant exits. This curated reading list targets ambitious entrepreneurs seeking practical frameworks proven to solve recurring business challenges at different growth stages.
