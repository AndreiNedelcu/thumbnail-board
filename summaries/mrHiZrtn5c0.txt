# Video Summary

This video documents the complete engine rebuild of a neglected 1997 Nissan 240SX that has sat dormant for nearly four years. The project centers on installing and restoring an RB25 engine—a legendary Nissan platform known for strong performance but notorious for oil starvation issues that cause catastrophic bearing failure. The creator methodically disassembles the engine, cleans components, inspects cylinder walls and pistons for damage, and performs precise measurements using micrometers and bore gauges to ensure proper bearing clearances and piston ring gap specifications. Key steps include deglazing cylinders with a ball hone tool, installing fresh piston rings, torquing main and rod bearings to exact specifications, and reassembling the rotating assembly. The video details technical troubleshooting throughout, addressing surface rust, carbon buildup, and wear patterns while maintaining a practical, educational tone. The target audience comprises car enthusiasts, particularly those interested in Japanese performance vehicles and engine rebuilding techniques, along with viewers who enjoy detailed mechanical documentation and problem-solving approaches in automotive restoration projects.
