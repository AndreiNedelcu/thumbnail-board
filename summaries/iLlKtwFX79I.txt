# WordPress Theme Selection Guide

The video addresses the critical mistake of choosing WordPress themes based solely on visual appearance rather than backend functionality. The creator explains that WordPress themes are complete ecosystems encompassing page builders, settings panels, and plugins—not just design templates. Poor theme selection leads to difficulty modifying pages, limited customization options, slow performance, and SEO penalties. The video targets WordPress website builders and small business owners seeking to avoid costly theme mistakes. Key recommendations include prioritizing backend editor functionality before aesthetics, selecting themes with popular page builders like Elementor or WP Bakery, and researching marketplace listings by examining sales volume, review ratings, and developer support before purchase. The creator highlights that theme refunds are rarely offered, making pre-purchase screening essential. For e-commerce sites, WooCommerce compatibility is critical. The presenter recommends specific themes including Salient (WP Bakery-based), Divi (proprietary builder with unlimited installations), and free options like the official WordPress themes. The video emphasizes that switching themes after building a site requires essentially rebuilding the entire website, underscoring the importance of making an informed initial choice.
