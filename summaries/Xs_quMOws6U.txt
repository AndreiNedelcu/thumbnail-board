# iOS 18 Home Screen Customization Summary

This video provides a comprehensive guide to customizing iPhone and iPad home screens with iOS 18's new features. The main topics covered include placing app icons freely anywhere on the home screen rather than in fixed grids, enlarging icons to remove app titles for a cleaner aesthetic, and using light/dark mode toggle options for icons. A significant focus is placed on icon tinting, both through manual color selection and by using a color dropper tool to extract colors from wallpapers. The guide extensively explores third-party widgets that enable advanced customization, including MD Studio for weather and calendar widgets, Dummi for creating minimal text-based app launchers similar to Light Phone, and Up Ahead for countdown timers to upcoming events. The video demonstrates multiple home screen setups across different focus modes, from minimal designs to information-rich layouts, and explains technical workarounds like screenshot-based transparent widgets. The content targets iPhone and iPad users interested in aesthetic home screen design and productivity organization, demonstrating that iOS 18 is currently available in public beta with full release coming fall 2024.
