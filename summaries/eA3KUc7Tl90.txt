The creator documents their experience entering the SMA19 Challenge, a no-dialogue manga competition with the theme "overflowing tears," as a way to reignite their passion for art. They detail the entire creative process: developing a concept inspired by "In Time" and "They Both Die at the End" about two boys discovering one has only 30 minutes left to live, designing characters, writing scripts, creating thumbnails, sketching, inking, and applying manga tones. Key challenges included fighting motivation loss during the lengthy sketching phase, learning Clip Studio Paint, and unexpectedly struggling with file compression during submission—files had to be under 2MB each, with the largest exceeding 10MB. The video targets aspiring manga artists and creative professionals curious about competition-based art projects, illustrating both the emotional and technical hurdles of manga production. The creator successfully completes their debut manga titled "A Beautiful Day to Die," then narrates the wordless story to help viewers understand the narrative, emphasizing lessons about maintaining personal commitments to build confidence.
