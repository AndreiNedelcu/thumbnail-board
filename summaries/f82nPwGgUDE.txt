# Summary

This video compilation features over 30 watch content creators sharing their most frequently worn watches throughout 2023 in 45-second segments. Participants discuss diverse timepieces ranging from luxury brands like Rolex, Patek Philippe, and Tag Heuer to accessible options from Seiko, Citizen, and Hamilton, explaining why each watch earned its place on their wrists during the year. Common themes include versatility for travel, comfort for daily wear, aesthetic appeal, reliable movements (both mechanical and quartz), and personal sentiment. Featured watches span multiple categories: sport divers like the Seiko Prospect and Tudor Black Bay, dress watches including Grand Seiko and Christopher Ward, tool watches such as the Rolex GMT-Master II, and unique pieces like the FPJ Elegant titanium and Czapek timepieces. The creators emphasize practical considerations like water resistance, case size, strap compatibility, and finishing quality, alongside emotional connections such as wearing a watch on significant life events. This compilation targets watch enthusiasts and collectors seeking real-world perspective on daily wearers from respected voices in the horological community.
