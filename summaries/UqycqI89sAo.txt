# Metal Detecting 100 Miles of Beach

This video follows a creator on a 100-mile metal detecting journey along a beach, inspired by a childhood gift from his father. The adventure chronicles his search for valuable treasures while documenting the mundane reality of beach metal detecting: mostly trash, random single shoes, bottle caps, and occasional interesting finds like a mysterious metal brick, a gold ring, AirPods, and coins. The creator intersperses humor throughout, creating running gags about losing his companion "Dwayne The Rock Johnson" (a metallic rock), finding beer bottles near what appears to be a college campus, and his deteriorating morale as miles pass with minimal valuable discoveries. Along the way, he highlights the broader issue of ocean pollution and trash, connecting it to the TeamSeas cleanup initiative. The video targets a general entertainment audience interested in treasure hunting, adventure vlogging, and feel-good content. It concludes with the creator visiting a pawn shop to evaluate his findings, discovering the pawn shop won't purchase his items and pivoting to eBay instead. The sponsor integration and personal storytelling—culminating in a reflective message about hopefully inspiring his future child to metal detect in his flip-flops—adds emotional weight to what is ultimately a lighthearted exploration of beach finds.
