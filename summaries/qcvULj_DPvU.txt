This video presents a comprehensive rematch between Copic sketch markers and OhoO alcohol markers, three years after the creator's original comparison. The video employs multiple testing methodologies to ensure objectivity, including blind taste tests with three professional artists, range comparisons, ink capacity evaluations, tip durability assessments, real-world blending applications, and detailed price analysis. Key findings reveal that while Copic once dominated due to superior range and refillability, OhoO has dramatically expanded its color range from 120 to 481 colors and now offers refillable ink systems. Performance tests show nearly identical results in blending, saturation, and professional-quality output, with OhoO markers actually demonstrating superior tip durability and crisp accuracy over extended use compared to Copic's tendency to degrade. The most significant differentiator is affordability: OhoO markers cost approximately one-third the price per marker, with their complete 320-color Honolulu series available for around $244 versus comparable Copic sets costing significantly more. The creator ultimately awards victory to OhoO for matching Copic's quality while substantially outperforming it in accessibility, value, and durability, targeting both beginner and professional artists seeking high-quality markers without premium pricing.
