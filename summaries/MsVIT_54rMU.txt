This video documents a biohacking experiment where the creator receives injections of 300 million mesenchymal stem cells derived from young Swedish bone marrow directly into six major joints—both knees, both hips, and both shoulders—over two days at a wellness clinic in the Bahamas. His father receives 100 million stem cells injected intravenously as a systemic treatment. The procedure aims to repair joint damage, reduce inflammation, and reverse aging-related deterioration, targeting the creator's goal of rejuvenating his body back to age eighteen. Working with Dr. Steve Samson, a board-certified physician specializing in non-surgical orthopedics, the team uses ultrasound guidance to precisely deliver the cells, which function as "mobile paramedics" that release anti-inflammatory signaling molecules. The protocol is part of a Phase 1 and 2A safety and efficacy study. The video targets biohacking enthusiasts and longevity seekers interested in cutting-edge regenerative medicine, documenting both the technical medical procedures and the creator's immediate post-injection experience, including inflammation, reduced mobility, and elevated heart rate as his body responds to the treatment.
