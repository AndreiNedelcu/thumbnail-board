# Summary

This video presents a sales consultation between Jeremy Miner and Scott, a 51-year-old police officer from Alaska with 29+ years of law enforcement experience who recently had his gang task force position eliminated. Scott discusses his desire for independence, higher income ($250,000+ annually versus his current $180,000 combined household income), work-from-home flexibility, and reduced financial stress affecting his health. Miner guides Scott through a discovery process exploring his frustrations with traditional employment, retirement concerns, and aspirations to travel and fund his seven children's education. Miner then presents a business opportunity involving a four-step sales system where a phone sales team generates leads and closes sales, positioning it as requiring minimal employee management while enabling significant income potential. The conversation demonstrates Miner's consultation technique emphasizing consultative questioning rather than product pitching, with the session conducted live before an audience of 160+ trainees at an event. The video concludes with Miner explaining his sales methodology to the audience, emphasizing how he never explicitly recommended the business—allowing the prospect to "sell himself" through strategic questioning.
