Two-time world rally champion Kalle Rovanperä, a 24-year-old Finnish driver, takes on the challenge of learning to drive a Formula 1 car at the Red Bull Ring with only ten laps to master it. The video documents his progression through increasingly advanced single-seater racing cars, starting with an F4 vehicle to learn the track layout, then moving to a Formula Renault to develop higher-speed racing skills. Throughout the experience, professional instructor Patrick provides guidance via radio, coaching Rovanperä on racing lines, throttle management, braking points, and car control techniques specific to open-wheel racing—a discipline distinctly different from his rally racing background, which emphasizes different car control methods. The video showcases the technical aspects of each vehicle, including gear systems, clutch usage, and tire temperature requirements. By his final lap in the F1 car, Rovanperä demonstrates significant improvement despite limited preparation time, adapting well to the extreme speed and G-forces of formula racing. The content appeals to motorsport enthusiasts interested in cross-discipline racing challenges and skill transfer between different racing formats.
