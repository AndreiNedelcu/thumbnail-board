This episode of a weekly tech news show covers multiple technology topics and industry trends of interest to gaming and hardware enthusiasts. The hosts also address infrastructure challenges from Ubiquiti UniFi security vulnerabilities and patches that degraded network performance during the event, requiring hardware upgrades to restore functionality.
