# Summary

This video provides an in-depth comparison of four compact action cameras: the GoPro Hero 13 Black, GoPro Hero 12 Black, DJI Osmo Action 4, and Insta360 Ace Pro, all priced under $400. The reviewer evaluates image quality across multiple dimensions including resolution, HDR capabilities, log color profiles, slow-motion performance, stabilization, and low-light performance. Key distinctions include the Insta360's superior 8K capability and low-light performance, the GoPro Hero 13's new macro and ultra-wide interchangeable lenses and magnetic quick-release mount, and the DJI's excellent low-light handling and front-facing touchscreen. The analysis covers physical characteristics, interface design, battery life testing, audio quality with built-in and wireless microphones, and mounting compatibility. The reviewer concludes that all four cameras deliver quality footage for different use cases: the Hero 13 for feature-rich control and GPS, the DJI for low-light versatility and touchscreen convenience, and the Ace Pro for superior low-light performance, while noting that upcoming updated models from DJI and Insta360 warrant consideration before purchasing.
