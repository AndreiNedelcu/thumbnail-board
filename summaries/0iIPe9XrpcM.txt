Additional observations include a potential dedicated Apple Intelligence button, matte finishes across models, and notably increased height dimensions for the Pro models compared to their predecessors, though width remains largely unchanged. The video targets Apple enthusiasts and potential iPhone buyers interested in early design leaks and detailed hardware analysis.
