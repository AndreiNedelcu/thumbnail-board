# Summary

This video documents a homeowner's journey fixing a problematic smart home after two years of renovations plagued by buggy GE Jasco smart switches that crippled his Z-Wave network. The primary issue was unreliable passive infrared motion sensors that malfunctioned frequently, created safety hazards with three-way switches, and inflexible automation settings. The creator introduces Inavi's new smart switches featuring millimeter-wave presence detection technology, which can sense people even when stationary through breathing detection, addressing the limitations of outdated infrared sensors. The video sponsors Homie Pro, a smart hub supporting both Z-Wave and Zigbee protocols with a user-friendly interface, demonstrating its setup process and automation capabilities called "flows." Throughout the testing, the creator pairs Inavi switches and Bailey motorized blinds to the Homie system, showcasing how the millimeter-wave sensors accurately detect presence with impressive sensitivity. While the technology remains in alpha stage with minor quirks, the overall setup proves significantly more reliable than the previous configuration. The content targets smart home enthusiasts and tech-savvy homeowners interested in home automation solutions, Z-Wave mesh networking, and practical comparisons between competing smart home platforms and sensor technologies.
