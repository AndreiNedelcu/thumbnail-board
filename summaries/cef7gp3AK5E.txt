# Summary

This prank video follows a YouTuber orchestrating an elaborate surprise where a friend is blindfolded for 10 hours and transported across Paris without knowing his destination. The friend, Leo, is given ear defenders playing jazz music and taken on various modes of transportation—taxis, trains, roller coasters, and escalators—while visiting iconic Parisian landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Arc de Triomphe. Throughout the journey, bystanders are in on the secret, and the friend makes incorrect guesses about his location, even believing at one point he's been taken to England. After the extended sensory deprivation experience, the blindfold is finally removed in a room where professional footballer Kylian Mbappé is waiting to meet him. The video includes a sponsorship integration for So Rare, a fantasy football card game, and concludes with the emotional reunion between Leo and Mbappé, who presents him with a signed photo and merchandise. The prank showcases the contrast between Leo's confused anticipation and the reality of meeting one of the world's best footballers, targeting fans of both prank content and football culture.
