# Summary

This video introduces XREAL's new Air 2 and Air 2 Pro AR glasses, featuring an interview with XREAL co-founder PJ. The glasses function as portable 13-inch displays that connect to devices via USB-C, mirroring content from phones, laptops, gaming consoles, and drones. Key improvements over the original Air include 25% brighter micro-OLED displays (500 nits), 10% lighter and thinner design, enhanced comfort through redistributed weight and softer nose pads, flexible arms, and significantly upgraded stereo speakers with directional audio. The Air 2 Pro's standout feature is electrochromic dimming technology, which electronically darkens the lenses to reduce glare and improve visibility in bright sunlight while maintaining peripheral awareness. Both models offer 1080p resolution, 120Hz refresh rates via air casting, individual color calibration, and certification for flicker-free eye comfort. The glasses support prescription lens accessories and come with improved light-blocking shades. Use cases include travel, entertainment, gaming, and work. The optional Xbeam accessory enables additional spatial display modes. The Air 2 costs £399 and the Air 2 Pro £449, launching in early November 2024.
