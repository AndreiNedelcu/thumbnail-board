This review examines the PowerBeats Pro 2, positioning them as superior workout earbuds that outperform AirPods Pro in several areas. The reviewer discusses the headphones' key improvements over the previous generation, including active noise cancellation, transparency mode, and heart rate tracking via an optical sensor borrowed from Apple Watch technology. The design features ear hooks that ensure secure fit during exercise, come in multiple sizes, and are 20 percent lighter than predecessors. Sound quality is characterized as energetic and bass-forward, suited for workout music genres. The H2 chip enables personalized spatial audio and seamless Apple ecosystem integration. The case has shrunk 33 percent while gaining USB-C, wireless charging, and increased battery capacity offering three full charges. Battery life reaches approximately a month with daily two-hour workouts. The reviewer targets fitness enthusiasts and Apple ecosystem users, arguing these $250 earbuds deliver exceptional value compared to competitors despite their premium pricing, while also noting that previous-generation models at lower prices remain viable alternatives.
