# Samsung QN900D 8K TV Flagship Review

This video presents an unboxing and detailed examination of Samsung's top-tier 85-inch QN900D 8K television paired with the Q990D soundbar system. The display features Samsung's latest NQ8 AI Gen 3 processor, which uses artificial intelligence upscaling to enhance lower-resolution content—claimed to make 4K footage look 90% as good as native 8K. Key design innovations include an ultra-slim profile comparable to a pencil thickness, a separate One Connect box housing ports for cleaner aesthetics, and Q Symphony audio integration that combines the TV's built-in speakers with external speakers for immersive surround sound. The display boasts a 240Hz refresh rate suitable for gaming, minimal bezel design, and support for native 8K content from sources like YouTube. The reviewer demonstrates the TV's capabilities by displaying both native 8K footage and upscaled 4K content, emphasizing the importance of screen size and viewing distance to fully appreciate the extra pixels. The video targets tech enthusiasts, home theater consumers, and potential buyers interested in premium television technology and how AI-driven upscaling can compensate for limited native 8K content availability.
