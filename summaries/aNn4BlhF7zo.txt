# Summary

This video provides a comprehensive one-month review comparing Apple's new M4 Mac Mini against its M2 predecessor, M4 Pro variant, and Mac Studio with M2 Ultra. The reviewer tests all four systems across multiple benchmarks including Geekbench, Cinebench, Blender rendering, and real-world applications like Photoshop, Premier Pro, and DaVinci Resolve, demonstrating that the base M4 Mac Mini offers 40-70% performance gains while the M4 Pro delivers even more significant improvements, particularly for GPU-intensive tasks. Key upgrades include increased base memory to 16GB (from 8GB), support for three external displays across all models, Thunderbolt 5 on Pro versions, and a more compact redesign. The reviewer emphasizes that at $599, the base M4 model represents exceptional value and future-proofing for most users, though the M4 Pro is worthwhile for content creators. The video targets Mac enthusiasts, professionals considering upgrades, and those building affordable desktop setups, highlighting the Mac Mini's versatility as a compact, powerful alternative to larger desktops or expensive iMacs.
