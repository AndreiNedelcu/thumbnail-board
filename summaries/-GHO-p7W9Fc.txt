# Devon Larratt: World Champion Armwrestler Training Secrets

This video documents a training session with Devon Larratt, widely regarded as the world's strongest armwrestler, at his home in Ottawa. Magnus Midtbø explores the technical and physical aspects of armwrestling, revealing it to be far more than raw strength—it's a highly technical sport similar to martial arts like jiu-jitsu. Key techniques discussed include rising (lifting knuckles to apply pressure on opponent's fingers), pronation (rotating the wrist outward), and maintaining proper arm positioning. Larratt demonstrates specialized training methods using custom-built armwrestling tables, cables, weights, and unique exercises targeting specific muscles like the pronator. The video also covers Larratt's background—he began armwrestling with his grandmother at age five and later served in Canada's Special Forces. His sons, Miles and potentially other family members, are training to follow in his footsteps. The content targets fitness enthusiasts, martial artists, and combat sports fans interested in specialized strength training, technical skill development, and the science behind elite athletic performance in niche sports.
