Ellen DeGeneres attempted a comeback with a Netflix standup special titled "For Your Approval," but it has been widely panned by critics. The special heavily focuses on addressing her cancelled reputation, which began in 2020 when a viral tweet prompted former employees and audience members to share accusations of mean behavior and creating a toxic work environment on her daytime talk show. Multiple accounts detailed her alleged rudeness to staff and audience members, contradicting her famous "be kind" messaging. Following these revelations, her show experienced a 43% viewer decline, ultimately leading to its cancellation in 2022. In the special, DeGeneres makes self-aware jokes about her fall from grace while also including out-of-touch segments about luxury car features. Critics have sharply divided on the performance: some view her unapologetic stance as refreshingly honest, while others criticize it as disingenuous image rehabilitation that fails to deliver genuine apologies or accountability. The special averages 5.4 stars on audience ratings, with nearly equal numbers of one and five-star reviews, reflecting the polarized discourse surrounding her attempted reinvention.
