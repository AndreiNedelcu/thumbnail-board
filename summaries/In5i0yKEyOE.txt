This video ranks all ten Formula 1 team driver lineups for the 2025 season, evaluating them purely on driver quality regardless of car performance. A panel of racing experts used an F1 points system to assess each pairing, with eight of the ten teams fielding new driver combinations. The ranking begins with AlphaTauri's Yuki Tsunoda and Isack Hadjar at the bottom, citing Hadjar's last-minute promotion as a significant liability, and progresses through teams like Alpine, Aston Martin, and Haas in the middle tiers. The video highlights the improvements of Sauber with Nico Hulkenberg and Gabriel Bortoletto, and praises Williams's massive upgrade through Carlos Sainz's arrival alongside Alex Albon. Red Bull's Max Verstappen and Liam Lawson rank fourth, with Mercedes's George Russell and Kimi Antonelli third. McLaren's Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri claim second place, while Ferrari's Charles Leclerc and Lewis Hamilton top the rankings despite questions about Hamilton's recent form and adaptation to Ferrari.
