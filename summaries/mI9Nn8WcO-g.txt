# Summary

This video documents a visit to the basement gym of Eve Scel, a professional climber and grip strength world record holder who has become an internet sensation for his extraordinary feats of strength. The video showcases Scel's unique combination of abilities: he holds multiple class and all-time records in grip strength competitions while also being a professional V15 climber, a rare skill fusion that makes him arguably the strongest climber in the world. Beyond his athletic achievements, Scel is also a special effects artist specializing in hyperrealistic silicone prosthetics for the film industry. The host documents Scel's training methods using specialized equipment including custom-made tiny edges (four millimeters), a flywheel machine with eccentric overload capability, a linear encoder for velocity-based training, and an on-site climbing wall featuring extremely challenging boulder problems. Throughout the session, Scel demonstrates record-breaking feats on various grip implements and climbing challenges, often casually accomplishing what appears nearly impossible. The video targets fitness enthusiasts, climbers, and strength athletes interested in cutting-edge training methodologies and human performance limits.
