# Summary

This video follows a creator's challenge to collect phone numbers from 100 celebrities across various high-profile events and locations. Starting with attempts at major venues like Power Slap events and movie premieres, the creator uses a novelty flip phone as a conversation starter, seeking celebrity contact information with plans to give away the "trap phone" to a subscriber at the video's end. The challenge takes him through Las Vegas casinos during a UFC fight at the Sphere, Halloween parties in Los Angeles, Formula 1 races in Austin, exclusive nightclubs, and celebrity dinners, where he encounters figures from entertainment, sports, music, and streaming communities including boxers, DJs, musicians, and content creators. Throughout the journey, the creator partners with a promoter named Ryan to gain access to exclusive events and high-limit casino rooms. The video emphasizes the difficulty of the task, featuring both successful interactions and rejections, while documenting the creator's attempts to befriend celebrities through genuine conversation despite the unconventional nature of his request. The format combines comedy, social experiment elements, and genuine networking, targeting audiences interested in celebrity encounters and entertainment industry access.
