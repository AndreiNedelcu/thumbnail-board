This video provides a comprehensive guide to digital file organization and naming conventions. The creator explains practical approaches to naming files effectively, emphasizing the choice between date-primary or keyword-primary naming systems, recommending the international standard date format (YYYY-MM-DD) paired with short, descriptive keywords and version modifiers. The content covers folder structure strategies progressing from general to specific categories, special character avoidance for cross-platform compatibility, and web-specific naming conventions using dashes instead of spaces or underscores. Additionally, the video introduces the PARA method (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archive) developed by Tiago Forte as an alternative organizational framework that prioritizes actionable items. The presenter provides three practical examples: business file organization with year-specific expenses and invoices, YouTube project structure with assets and dated folders, and a simplified podcast episode system. The video targets creative professionals, content creators, and anyone struggling with disorganized digital file systems, offering scalable solutions adaptable to different platforms and workflow needs while emphasizing the importance of consistency and system adherence.
