# Summary

This video documents a year-long social experiment where the creator offers a stranger $50,000 to start a business, testing whether they will invest it responsibly or misuse the opportunity. He meets Daisy on the streets of Hong Kong and learns she dreams of opening a pastry shop. After she impressively delivers homemade cakes within 48 hours, he commits to funding her venture with an initial $110,000 to quit her job, plus an additional $50,000 in business investment. Throughout the following months, Daisy's business gains viral attention and significant customer growth through social media. The video then documents her introduction to fifty Hong Kong entrepreneurs at a launch event, where her products again receive strong validation. The experiment concludes with the creator revealing that his company earned $25,000 from the viral video and donates it entirely to Daisy as additional funding. The content targets entrepreneurs, business enthusiasts, and viewers interested in human behavior, risk-taking, and real-world startup stories, blending elements of social experimentation with inspirational business content.
