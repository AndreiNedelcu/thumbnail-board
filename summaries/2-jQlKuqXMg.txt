# AirPods 4 vs AirPods Pro 2 Comparison

This video provides a comprehensive comparison between three AirPods models: the base AirPods 4 ($130), AirPods 4 with active noise cancellation ($180), and AirPods Pro 2 ($190). The reviewer examines sound quality, fit, noise cancellation performance, find my capabilities, and charging options across all three models. Sound quality is comparable between both AirPods 4 variants due to their identical 11mm drivers, though the AirPods Pro 2 deliver superior bass due to their ear-canal sealing silicone tips. The key differentiator is noise cancellation: while the base AirPods 4 offer no active noise cancellation, the ANC version effectively reduces white noise but allows more ambient sounds through than the AirPods Pro 2, which provide near-complete isolation. Additional differences include find my tracking (UWB precision on Pro 2 vs. Bluetooth proximity on ANC AirPods 4), charging methods (wireless charging on ANC version only), and case design. The video targets consumers deciding between these models, offering guidance based on personal preferences regarding in-ear comfort, noise cancellation priorities, and budget considerations.
