# Summary

This investigation exposes the CryptoZoo NFT project as an elaborate scheme involving Logan Paul and his team, including crypto influencer Jake "Crypto King," manager Jeff Levin, and developer Eddie Ibanez. Through leaked internal messages and blockchain analysis, the video reveals how the team conducted a stealth launch in June 2021, secretly accumulating tokens before publicly announcing the project in August, effectively creating an unlocked pre-sale disguised to avoid SEC scrutiny. The investigation demonstrates that team members engaged in suspicious trading patterns, with Crypto King purchasing significantly more tokens than disclosed in official records. When the official September launch occurred, Logan and Eddie mysteriously blacklisted Crypto King's wallets, excluding him from the airdrop of new tokens—prompting his public accusations that Logan stole $40 million. However, blockchain evidence shows Crypto King himself sold millions of dollars in Zoo tokens despite the supposed blacklist, making approximately six million dollars profit from a failed project. Similarly, Eddie Ibanez sold around 1.7 million dollars worth. The video targets crypto investors and those interested in NFT scams, revealing how insiders manipulated the project for personal gain while community members lost substantial investments in a non-functional product.
